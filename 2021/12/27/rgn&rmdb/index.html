New Columns Comming Soon

Since I’m an enthusiastic fan for games and movies, I choose to set up some columns talking about reviews for those works.

RMDb

Ricky’s Movie Database is a column where I share my reviews on films, television series, etc[1].

Hope you don’t mind, IMDb. XD

In this column, I will not only score those works with a grade between 0 and 10 in different aspects, but also write some short or long comments.

I will score those video works mainly from those aspects below:

  • Stories
  • Characters
  • Visual Effects
  • Conception

RGN

In Ricky’s Games Network, I will share my opinions on games that I have already played for a while. Just like RMDb, I will rate those games and write comments about them.

I will score those games mainly from those aspects below:

  • Stories&Charaters
  • Visual Effects
  • Conception
  • Playability
  • Friendly

Remember, those reviews are only personal opinions. I am not a professional reviewer, so please be patient and willing to share your view in the comment. I am looking forward to hearing your voice.
